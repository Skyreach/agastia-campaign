# Quest: [[The Codex]] Search

## Player Summary

[[Manny]] (#1) and [[Nikki]] (#2), the two newest subjects of the [[Decimate Project]], have been dispatched by [[Professor Zero]] to locate and retrieve the **Dominion Evolution Codex**, an ancient tome of immense power. [[The Codex]]'s true contents and purpose remain unknown, but [[Professor Zero]] considers it critically important to the Project's goals.

### Quest Objectives

**Primary Goal:**
- Locate the Dominion Evolution Codex
- Retrieve the Codex for [[Professor Zero]]
- (Secret) Discover what the Codex actually contains
- (Secret) Question whether to deliver it to [[Professor Zero]]

**Investigation Path:**

**Known Leads:**
1. **[[Archive of Lost Histories]]** ([[Scholar Quarter]], Tier 3) - Historical records, ancient texts
2. **[[Stonemark Antiquities]]** ([[Merchant District]]) - Rare book dealer, black market connections
3. **[[Veridian (Scrollkeeper)]]** ([[Scholar Quarter]]) - Ancient elf scholar, knows [[Agastia]]'s entire history
4. **[[Meridian's Rest]]** Falls Area - Garrek's journal mentions "ancient book at the Falls"

**Mystery Questions:**
- What does the Codex actually contain?
- Why does [[Professor Zero]] want it?
- What is the true purpose of the [[Decimate Project]]?
- What happens if [[Manny]] and [[Nikki]] discover the truth about their pasts?

- Ancient tome of "evolution magic"
- Contains knowledge of transformation/change
- Connected to dominion over life and growth
- Potentially thousands of years old

**Physical Description (from legends):**
- Bound in unknown material (not leather, not metal)

## DM Notes

### Quest Design Philosophy

**Layered Mystery:**
- Surface: Find ancient book
- Layer 2: Why does [[Professor Zero]] want it?
- Layer 3: What are [[Manny]] and [[Nikki]]?
- Layer 4: What is the [[Decimate Project]]?
- Core: Campaign-level implications

**Player Agency:**
- No "correct" answer
- Multiple valid choices
- Consequences for all paths
- Character-driven decision

### Pacing

**Long-Term Quest:**
- Spans multiple sessions (2-10+)
- Background objective during other quests
- Incremental progress through investigation
- Major milestone when located

**Parallel Progression:**
- Can pursue while doing other quests
- [[Scholar Quarter]] access tied to Merit rank
- Research takes downtime between sessions
- Breakthrough moments in key sessions

### Integration with Other Quests

**[[Nikki]]'s Temporal Bloodline:**
- Codex may contain time magic
- Falls anomaly connected to bloodline
- [[Nona (Subject #9)]] knows about bloodline
- Temporal magic thread throughout campaign

**[[Josh]]'s Ancestral Markings:**
- Markings may be Codex-related protection
- Ancient order protected the Codex
- [[Josh]] descendants of protectors?
- Potential conflict with [[Manny]]/[[Nikki]]

**[[Professor Zero]] Arc:**
- Central to understanding main antagonist
- Reveals [[Decimate Project]] true purpose
- Sets up future confrontations
- Ties to campaign endgame

### [[The Codex]]'s True Nature (DM Options)

**Option 1: Decimate Formula**
- Contains method for memory wipe + enhancement
- [[Professor Zero]] wants to perfect the process
- Other subjects were experiments
- [[Manny]] and [[Nikki]] are test for new approach

**Option 2: Evolution Magic**
- Actual transformation/evolution spells
- [[Professor Zero]] seeks power/immortality
- [[Decimate Project]] subjects are ingredients
- Codex unlocks final ritual

**Option 3: World-Saving Knowledge**
- [[Professor Zero]] knows apocalypse is coming
- Codex contains prevention method
- Terrible means for good end
- Moral complexity for party

**Option 4: Beholder Connection**
- Codex is pre-beholder artifact
- [[Professor Zero]] works for/with beholder
- Subjects are future beholder servants
- Ties to larger cosmic threat

**Option 5: Red Herring**
- Codex is powerful but not what [[Professor Zero]] really wants
- Real goal is observing [[Manny]]/[[Nikki]]
- Quest itself is the experiment
- True objective is measuring their choices
