# Legendary Location Generator

## Purpose
Generate richly detailed locations using the "Legendary Locations" format - combining evocative description, clear mechanics, and balanced risk/reward. Based on the format from "Legendary Locations" sourcebook.

## When to Use
- Creating memorable setpiece locations for sessions
- Adapting published "Legendary Locations" content to campaign
- Designing high-impact areas with mechanical effects
- Building locations that reward exploration and investigation

## Format Structure

A Legendary Location follows this template:

### 1. Lore Introduction
Short paragraph (2-4 sentences) establishing:
- **Name and Origin** - Who discovered/named it, when
- **Reputation** - What is it known for? Who knows about it?
- **The Duality** - Balance of "what can help can also harm"
- **The Temptation** - Why people seek it despite danger

### 2. Evocative Description
Read-aloud text focusing on:
- **Sensory Details** - Sound, sight, smell (be specific)
- **The Draw** - What beckons characters forward?
- **Visual Phenomenon** - Unique/magical/strange elements
- **Hidden Details** - Clues revealed with investigation

**Format:**
```
> [Read-aloud box with rich sensory description]
```

### 3. Mechanics Section
Clear mechanical effects with:
- **Passive Environmental Effects** - What happens just by being there?
- **Skill Check Reveals** - DC + skill + what is learned
- **Investigation Rewards** - Information for engaging with location

**Example:**
```
## Mechanics

### [Effect Name]
As soon as a creature enters this area, it [mechanical effect]. A DC [number] [Ability] ([Skill]) check will reveal [hidden detail or cause].

### [Investigation Detail]
A DC [number] [Ability] ([Skill]) check will reveal [specific discovery].
```

### 4. Key Dangers
The central threat/curse/challenge with:
- **Dramatic Name** - Bold subheading that warns players
- **Clear Trigger** - What activates the danger?
- **Mechanical Effect** - Specific save DC, consequences, duration
- **Cure/Solution** - How to overcome or remove effect

**Example:**
```
## Key Dangers

### [Danger Name in Bold]
[Description of the danger and why it matters]

**Mechanical Effect:**
- [Specific trigger condition]
- [Save DC and type]
- [Consequence of failure]
- [How to remove/cure effect]
```

### 5. Potential Loot or Boons
Specific, powerful rewards:
- **Proportional to Risk** - Greater danger = greater reward
- **Concrete Effects** - Specific mechanical benefits (not vague)
- **Limited Use** - Single use, charges, conditions
- **Legendary Quality** - Effects that feel special/unique

**Example:**
```
## Potential Loot or Boons

### [Reward Name]
[Specific item or effect] will:
- [Mechanical benefit 1]
- [Mechanical benefit 2]
- [Mechanical benefit 3]
- [Any conditions or limits]
```

## Campaign Integration (DM Notes)

After the core Legendary Location format, add campaign-specific details:

### Connection to Campaign
- How does this tie to ongoing quests/factions?
- What secrets does it reveal about the world?
- How can it affect character arcs?

### Faction/Guardian Details
- Who protects or controls this location?
- What are their motivations?
- How do they interact with visitors?

### Adventure Hooks
- How to introduce the location
- Potential conflicts or complications
- Connections to other content

### Encounter Scaling
- Appropriate party level
- Stat blocks for guardians/creatures
- Alternative encounters based on party choices

## Example Usage

**User Request:**
"I need a legendary location for a desert oasis that grants visions but causes madness."

**Agent Response:**
1. **Lore Introduction** - Establish the Oracle's Pool discovered by ancient seers
2. **Description** - Shimmering water, mirage-like visions in steam, scent of myrrh
3. **Mechanics** - Drinking grants *Divination* effect (DC 15 Arcana to understand)
4. **Key Danger** - "Visions of What Must Not Be" - DC 13 Wisdom save or gain short-term madness
5. **Boons** - One question answered truthfully about past, present, or future
6. **DM Notes** - Connect to prophecy faction, guardian sphinx, plot hooks

## Quality Checklist

Before presenting a Legendary Location, verify:

- [ ] **Evocative Name** - Memorable and descriptive
- [ ] **Sensory Description** - At least 3 senses engaged
- [ ] **Clear Mechanics** - Specific DCs and effects
- [ ] **Risk/Reward Balance** - Danger proportional to benefit
- [ ] **Investigation Rewards** - Skill checks reveal details
- [ ] **Cure Exists** - Players can overcome dangers
- [ ] **Campaign Integration** - Connects to existing lore
- [ ] **Multiple Approaches** - Combat, negotiation, stealth all viable
