## Description

### Exterior

**Approach:**
Overgrown path leads through what were once manicured gardens. Stone walls crumble, gates hang crooked on rusted hinges. Statues weather-worn and moss-covered. Trees and vines reclaim the grounds. Eerie silence broken only by wind through ruins and occasional bird calls.

**Main Structure:**
Three-story mansion (once impressive) now gutted by fire. Outer walls partially standing, blackened by smoke and soot. Roof completely collapsed. Windows are empty sockets. Grand entrance partially obstructed by fallen masonry. West wing more intact than east wing (fire's origin point?).

**Grounds:**
- **Gardens:** Overgrown, some exotic plants gone wild
- **Stables:** Partially burned, still smells of old smoke and death
- **Servants' Quarters:** Separate building, less damaged
- **Carriage House:** Roof intact, doors blocked
- **Well:** Still functional (water quality unknown)
- **Family Crypt:** Underground, sealed iron door

**Current Hazards:**
- Unstable floors and ceilings (collapse risk)
- Hidden holes and pits from fire damage
- Rusty nails and sharp debris
- Possible squatters or monsters
- Lingering magical effects (if fire was magical)

### Interior (What Remains)

**Ground Floor:**
- **Grand Foyer:** Charred chandelier crashed on floor, grand staircase partially intact but unstable
- **Ballroom:** Mostly collapsed, debris everywhere, murals smoke-damaged beyond recognition
- **Dining Room:** Table burned to ash, servant's door leads to kitchen ruins
- **Library:** Worst damage (many books fuel fire?), some volumes in iron safe might survive
- **Study:** Partially intact, desk and filing cabinet buried under debris

**Second Floor:**
- Bedrooms along corridor (most floors collapsed into ground level)
- Master Suite at end (partially accessible via dangerous walk on beams)
- Nursery (if family had children)
- Guest rooms (varying damage)
- All furniture burned or ruined by weather

**Third Floor / Attic:**
- Almost completely collapsed
- Storage areas might have survived in corners
- Servants' quarters at this level
- Access extremely dangerous

**Basement:**
- Wine cellar (stone construction, likely intact)
- Storage rooms
- Possible secret areas (hidden by noble family)
- Access via collapsed kitchen or external entrance
