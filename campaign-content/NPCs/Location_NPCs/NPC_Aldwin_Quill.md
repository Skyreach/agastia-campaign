# Aldwin Quill

## Player Summary

**Aldwin Quill** is a gnome scribe who operates a scriptorium in [[Meridian's Rest]], crafting spell scrolls, copying documents, and providing literacy services to the community. Grateful to the party for clearing the Ratterdan corruption, he offers discounted scroll services and materials to those who helped protect the town.

### Basic Information
- **Race:** Gnome (rock)
- **Gender:** Male
- **Age:** Middle-aged (65, young for a gnome)
- **Occupation:** Scribe / Scroll Crafter
- **Location:** [[Meridian's Rest]] (Scriptorium)
- **Personality:** Meticulous, bookish, grateful, detail-oriented

### Appearance
- Small gnome with tidy brown hair and spectacles
- Ink-stained fingers (permanent)
- Well-tailored vest with many pockets for quills and tools
- Magnifying lenses on adjustable headband for fine work
- Usually surrounded by papers, scrolls, and books
- Speaks precisely, avoids contractions

## Services Offered

### Standard Services
**Document Work:**
- Letter writing: 1cp per page
- Document copying: 5cp per page
- Official paperwork: 1sp (with wax seal)
- Translation services: 1gp per page (knows Common, Gnomish, Dwarven, Elvish)

**Scroll Crafting (for Casters):**
- **Cantrip Scroll:** 1 day, 15gp
- **1st-level Scroll:** 1 day, 25gp
- **2nd-level Scroll:** 3 days, 100gp
- **3rd-level Scroll:** 5 days, 250gp

**Materials (15-20% Discount for Party):**
- Fine parchment (10 sheets): 1gp → 8sp
- Quality ink (bottle): 10gp → 8gp
- Sealing wax: 5sp → 4sp
- Spell component pouch refills: Varies

### Crafting Requirements
**For Spell Scrolls:**
- Caster must provide the spell (knows it or has it prepared)
- Aldwin provides materials and scribing expertise
- Time varies by spell level
- Discount applies to material costs only

## Knowledge & Information

### Literacy & Education
- One of few literate people in [[Meridian's Rest]]
- Reads and writes letters for townsfolk
- Teaches basic literacy (for fee or favor)
- Knows multiple languages

### Records & History
- Maintains town records (births, deaths, property)
- Has access to historical documents
- Can research questions using his library
- Knows local genealogies and land ownership

### Scroll Magic Theory
- Understands arcane theory (non-caster)
- Can explain spell scroll mechanics
- Knows which spells are commonly useful
- Can recommend scrolls for specific situations

## Relationships

### Fellow Merchants
- **[[Greta Moss]]:** Friendly professional relationship
  - Refers customers to each other
  - Shares tea and discusses crafts
  - Trades materials occasionally (ink for rare herbs)

### Town Officials
- **[[Brian Thornscale]]:** Official scribe for town documents
  - Maintains official records
  - Handles legal paperwork
  - Paid retainer by town

### Adventurers
- **Party:** Grateful for clearing corruption
  - Offers 15-20% discount on materials
  - Willing to craft scrolls during downtime
  - Can research questions using his library

### Casters
- Friendly with traveling wizards and clerics
- Often copies spells for wizards (for fee)
- Exchanges magical theory discussion

## DM Notes

### Aldwin's Role
- **Scroll Crafter:** Provides spell scroll access during downtime
- **Information Source:** Has records and books for research
- **Literacy Services:** Handles document needs
- **Recurring Contact:** Available for future scroll needs

### Why the Discount?
- Town is grateful for corruption clearing
- Aldwin personally appreciates improved safety
- Corruption threatened his paper and ink supplies
- Goodwill toward capable adventurers

### Personality Notes
- Meticulous and detail-oriented
- Speaks precisely (gnome trait)
- Knowledge-focused (loves learning)
- Fair prices, honest dealings
- Generous to town protectors
- Bookish but not antisocial

### Scroll Crafting Mechanics
- Uses standard D&D scroll crafting rules
- Requires caster to know/provide spell
- Discount applies to material costs only (not time)
- Party can commission scrolls during downtime periods

### Research Services
**Aldwin's Library:**
- Small personal collection (100-200 books)
- Regional history, magical theory, genealogy
- Can research questions (takes time)
- Charges 5gp per day of research + results

**Examples:**
- "What do records say about Garrek?" → 1 day research
- "Are there maps of Underdark near here?" → 2 day research
- "What's known about temporal magic?" → 3 day research

### Future Hooks
- Regular scroll supplier
- Can translate ancient documents
- May need rare ink ingredients gathered (quest hook)
- Connection to wizards and magical knowledge
- Can identify magical writing

## Secrets
**Minor Secret:** Quietly investigating a historical mystery
- Found reference to ancient artifact in old documents
- Hasn't told anyone (worried about treasure hunters)
- Would share with trusted scholars

## Combat Stats
**None.** Aldwin is a non-combatant.

**If Shop Attacked:**
- Flees or hides (no combat capability)
- May use minor illusion or prestidigitation (knows cantrips from study, not a caster)
- Relies on town guard for protection
