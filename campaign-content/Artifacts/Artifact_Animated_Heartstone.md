# [[Animated Heartstone]]

## Player Summary

The **Animated Heartstone** is a powerful fey artifact discovered in the shadow-corrupted dungeon beneath [[Ratterdan]] during Session 1. When the party retrieved it, the crystalline heart burst with purple light, granting each party member a permanent feat based on their deepest desires. The artifact then transformed into an animated form and went dormant, though its nature and future potential remain mysterious.

The Heartstone broke the corruption afflicting the **Giant's Axe**, allowing [[Ian/Rakash]] to claim the weapon. An evil, echoing laugh accompanied its activation â€” suggesting an unknown mastermind behind its placement (later revealed to be [[Octavia (Subject #8)]], Subject #8 of the [[Decimate Project]]).

**Critical Warning:** If the Heartstone is destroyed, the party will LOSE the feats it granted.

- **Feat Granting:** Granted each PC a permanent feat (one-time effect)
- **Curse Breaking:** Broke the corruption on the Giant's Axe
- **Animated Form:** Transformed into living creature (shape TBD during session)
- **Fey Origin:** Strong fey aura, transmutation school magic

- **Fragile Power:** Destroying the Heartstone removes granted feats
