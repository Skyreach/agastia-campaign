# Quest: The Giant's Axe

## Player Summary

[[Ian/Rakash]], a goblin barbarian, feels an overwhelming compulsion toward a massive giant's axe discovered in the [[Ratterdan Ruins]]. When touched, the weapon transforms and bonds to him, revealing itself as a sentient artifact. The axe carries the rage and memory of slain giants, and its ultimate purpose is to take revenge on [[Krythak Stormbringer]] and the [[Storm Giant Warband]] who once wielded it.

### Quest Objectives

**Stage 1: Claiming the Axe (Session 1)**
- Cleanse the [[Animated Heartstone]] corruption in [[Ratterdan Ruins]]
- Touch the giant's axe when the compulsion comes
- Bond with the weapon (transformation)

**Stage 2: Understanding the Axe (Early Campaign)**
- Learn the axe's history
- Discover why a goblin can wield a giant's weapon
- Understand the axe's sentience and goals
- Master the weapon's growing powers

**Stage 3: The Storm Giants (Mid Campaign)**
- Gather intelligence on [[Storm Giant Warband]]
- Learn about [[Krythak Stormbringer]]
- Track the giants' movements
- Build strength to face them

**Stage 4: Revenge (Late Campaign)**
- Confront [[Storm Giant Warband]]
- Face [[Krythak Stormbringer]]
- Fulfill the axe's purpose
- Choose whether to keep wielding the weapon after revenge

## The Giant's Axe (Artifact)

### Physical Properties

**Appearance (Before Bonding):**
- Massive weapon, too large for non-giant
- Ancient design, storm motifs
- Embedded in stone or displayed as trophy
- Faint lightning crackling on blade

**Appearance (After Bonding):**
- Transforms to fit [[Ian/Rakash]]'s size
- Still clearly giant-made but now wieldable
- Lightning patterns intensify
- Runes glow during combat

### Magical Properties

**Current Abilities (Level 1-4):**
- Functions as magical greatsword (1d6 slashing for Small creature)
- +1 to attack and damage (grows with [[Ian/Rakash]])
- Lightning damage bonus (1d4, increases with level)
- Sentience (can communicate with [[Ian/Rakash]])

**Future Abilities (Level 5-10+):**
- Enhanced lightning damage
- Thunder/storm powers
- Size alteration (return to giant form temporarily)
- Rage synergy (powers boost during barbarian rage)
- Giant-slaying properties

**Ultimate Power (Late Game):**
- Storm Giant-killer mode
- Summon storm/lightning
- Giant's strength temporarily
- Revenge strike against [[Krythak Stormbringer]]

### Sentience & Personality

**The Axe's Voice:**
- Speaks to [[Ian/Rakash]] telepathically
- Gruff, battle-hardened, vengeful
- Respects strength and courage
- Pushes [[Ian/Rakash]] toward confrontation

**The Axe's Goals:**
- Kill [[Krythak Stormbringer]]
- Destroy [[Storm Giant Warband]]
- Prove giants can fall to smaller warriors
- Redeem its loss (was taken as trophy)

**The Axe's History:**
- Forged by storm giants centuries ago
- Wielded by great giant warriors
- Lost in battle when wielder fell
- Taken as trophy by [[Krythak Stormbringer]]'s ancestors
- Eventually abandoned in [[Ratterdan Ruins]]
- Waited for worthy wielder

## Quest Stages

### Stage 1: The Calling (Session 1)

**Location:** [[Ratterdan Ruins]]
**Trigger:** Party explores ruins, finds shadow-corrupted heartstone

**The Compulsion:**
- [[Ian/Rakash]] feels pulled toward back chamber
- Sees massive axe displayed as trophy
- Overwhelming urge to touch it
- *"[To Rakash only]: You feel an overwhelming compulsion pulling you toward the weapon. Something deep within recognizes this axe as YOURS."*

**The Cleansing:**
- Party defeats shadow creatures
- Cleanses [[Animated Heartstone]] with fire/radiant damage
- Corruption lifts from ruins

**The Claiming:**
- [[Ian/Rakash]] approaches axe
- Touches weapon
- Transformation occurs:
  - Axe shrinks to fit him
  - Lightning surges through bond
  - Memories of giants flash through mind
  - Voice speaks: *"At last... a warrior worthy of wielding me."*

**Immediate Consequences:**
- [[Ian/Rakash]] bonded to artifact
- Weapon becomes primary equipment
- First hints of sentience
- Compulsion satisfied (for now)

### Stage 2: Understanding (Sessions 2-5)

**The Axe Speaks:**
- Periodic mental communication
- Reveals its purpose incrementally
- Demands [[Ian/Rakash]] grow stronger
- Hints at revenge against giants

**Investigation:**
- Identify weapon as artifact
- Research storm giant weapons
- Learn about [[Storm Giant Warband]]
- Discover [[Krythak Stormbringer]]'s name

**Growing Bond:**
- Axe powers increase with [[Ian/Rakash]]'s level
- Weapon approves of brave/strong actions
- Disapproves of cowardice or weakness
- Becomes more communicative over time

**Information Gathering (Session 2):**
- Thorne (caravan guard) tells war stories
- Mentions Storm Giants in region
- May recognize axe type
- Provides leads on giant locations

### Stage 3: The Hunt (Mid Campaign, Level 5-8)

**Tracking the Giants:**
- Learn [[Storm Giant Warband]] movements
- Discover their base or patrol routes
- Understand their hierarchy
- Identify [[Krythak Stormbringer]] as leader

**Building Strength:**
- [[Ian/Rakash]] levels up
- Axe powers grow
- Party becomes capable of giant combat
- Allies recruited for assault

**Intelligence Network:**
- Towns attacked by giants
- Survivors share information
- Giant activity patterns
- Weaknesses discovered

**Moral Questions:**
- Is revenge worth the risk?
- What if giants aren't purely evil?
- Will killing them satisfy [[Ian/Rakash]]?
- What happens to axe after revenge?

### Stage 4: The Confrontation (Late Campaign, Level 8-10+)

**Approaching [[Storm Giant Warband]]:**
- Locate giant stronghold or camp
- Plan assault strategy
- Face lesser giants first
- Build to [[Krythak Stormbringer]] fight

**Boss Fight: [[Krythak Stormbringer]]**
- Storm giant leader (CR 13+)
- Recognizes the axe
- Combat with lightning and storm powers
- Axe's abilities peak during this fight

**Critical Choice:**
- Kill [[Krythak Stormbringer]] (complete revenge)
- Spare him (mercy/different path)
- Negotiate (giants become allies?)
- Learn truth (were giants justified in original conflict?)

### Stage 5: After Revenge

**If [[Krythak Stormbringer]] Killed:**
- Axe's purpose fulfilled
- Weapon's personality changes (satisfied? empty?)
- [[Ian/Rakash]] must decide to keep or retire axe
- [[Storm Giant Warband]] remnants may seek revenge

**If Giants Spared:**
- Axe frustrated or understanding?
- Alternative resolution to conflict
- Giants become allies or recurring threat
- [[Ian/Rakash]] defines own path

**The Axe's Future:**
- Does it remain with [[Ian/Rakash]]?
- New purpose discovered?
- Passed to another warrior?
- Retired as monument?

## DM Notes

### Quest Design Philosophy

**Multi-Stage Epic:**
- Begins Level 1, culminates Level 10+
- Spans entire campaign
- Background objective, not urgent
- Allows organic development

**Character Integration:**
- Defines [[Ian/Rakash]]'s identity
- Signature weapon and story arc
- Connection to larger world (giants)
- Personal stakes in campaign

**Flexibility:**
- Can pursue when ready
- No forced timeline
- Parallel to other quests
- Player-driven pacing

### The Axe as NPC

**Sentient Weapon Roleplay:**
- Distinct personality (gruff warrior)
- Conversations during rests
- Comments on [[Ian/Rakash]]'s choices
- Relationship evolves

**Mechanical Integration:**
- Powers scale automatically with level
- Never becomes obsolete
- Signature weapon throughout campaign
- Special abilities tied to story beats

**Conflict Potential:**
- Axe may disagree with [[Ian/Rakash]]
- Pushes toward confrontation
- Tests [[Ian/Rakash]]'s independence
- Creates character moments

### Moral Complexity Options

**Simple Revenge:**
- Giants are antagonists
- Axe's revenge justified
- Clear good vs evil
- Satisfying conclusion

**Complex Revenge:**
- Giants have reasons for actions
- Axe biased/unreliable narrator
- [[Ian/Rakash]] must judge
- Mercy becomes option

**Tragic History:**
- Axe's original wielder was villain
- Giants were defending themselves
- Revenge is unjustified
- [[Ian/Rakash]] breaks cycle

**Alternative Truth:**
- [[Krythak Stormbringer]] didn't take axe
- Different giant responsible
- Real enemy yet to discover
- Quest continues differently
