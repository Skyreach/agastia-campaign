# Blades of Glory

## Overview

**Type & Specialty:** Premium magical arms and armor - Very Rare and Legendary enchanted weapons, armor, and custom commissions for elite warriors

**Location:** Research Quarter (Tier 2-3), elevated terrace overlooking the city, near Augury Spire

**Clientele:** Elite warriors, Council champions, high-level adventurers, noble house guards, heroes of renown

**Reputation:** "Where legends arm themselves." The finest magical weaponsmith in Agastia, perhaps the entire region. Every piece a masterwork combining enchantment theory with battlefield-tested practicality.

**Atmosphere:** More gallery than shop. Enchanted weapons displayed in warded alcoves, each lit to highlight craftsmanship. Soft magical lighting. Air charged with ambient arcane energy. Hushed conversations - this is sacred space for warriors who understand what they're viewing. Viewing areas separated from forge (audible rhythmic hammering and arcane chanting from below).

## Proprietor

### Master Enchanter Kaelith Brightspark
**Role:** Proprietor and master weaponsmith
**Faction:** Merit Council (Disposition: Aligned on principle, independent in practice)

**Background:**
- Tier 2 merit holder through magical innovation achievement
- Studied enchanting at Grand Academy, exceeded all instructors by age 25
- Spent decade traveling, studying different magical traditions and weapon styles
- Established Blades of Glory fifteen years ago with Council grant for excellence
- Created several magical weapons now legendary in their own right
- Personally fields-tests experimental designs (accomplished duelist despite scholarly appearance)

**Goals:**
- Short-term: Perfect shapeshifting enchantment allowing single weapon to take any form
- Long-term: Create enchantment that allows weapon to grow stronger alongside its wielder

**Personality:**
- Passionate craftsman who views each commission as personal masterpiece
- Perfectionist - won't release piece until it exceeds expectations
- Surprisingly humble given skill level - credits materials and wielders, not own genius
- Becomes animated discussing theory, loses track of time
- Respects warriors who truly understand their weapons

**Interaction Hooks:**
- Would seek player help with: Recovering rare materials from dangerous locations, field-testing experimental weapons, investigating rival enchanter's suspicious success
- Reluctance level: Willing (once players prove themselves worthy)
- Can offer players: Custom enchantments at cost, prototype weapons for testing, introductions to Council officials, magical theory consultation

**Party Connections:**
- Potential bonds with: Manny (fellow weapon specialist, understanding of practical combat needs), any PC with enchanting interest
- Relationship type: Master craftsman / Discerning client â†’ could evolve to Mentor / Professional respect

## Day-to-Day Operations

**Hours:**
- By appointment only (scheduled minimum 1 week advance for viewings)
- Forge operates all hours (Kaelith maintains unusual schedule driven by inspiration)
- Consultations scheduled midday (Kaelith most lucid after morning coffee)

**Typical Day:**
- Morning: Review overnight forge progress, quality inspections
- Midday: Client consultations, item demonstrations, commissions discussions
- Afternoon: Enchanting work requiring precision and focus
- Evening: Experimental designs, theoretical research, correspondence with other enchanters
- Night: Heavy forging work when inspiration strikes

**Access Requirements:**
1. **By Invitation:** Kaelith invites proven warriors of exceptional skill
2. **By Achievement:** Completion of major quest recognized by Council (level 10+ suggested)
3. **By Recommendation:** Three current clients vouch for potential customer
4. **By Purchase:** Minimum 1,000gp purchase just to browse (refunded toward first item)

**Regular Customers:**
- **Council Champions:** Elite warriors serving Merit Council directly
- **Noble House Guards:** Commanders protecting Tier 2-3 estates
- **Renowned Adventurers:** Those whose deeds warrant finest equipment
- **Master Duelists:** Competitors in high-society dueling circuits
- **Artifact Collectors:** Wealthy individuals acquiring legendary pieces

**Real Uses:**
- **Access to Legendary Items:** Only source in Agastia for Very Rare/Legendary magic weapons
- **Custom Commissions:** Work with master enchanter to create specific desired properties
- **Item Identification:** Kaelith automatically succeeds, provides complete item history
- **Restoration Services:** Repair legendary items other enchanters can't handle
- **Magical Theory:** Consultation on how enchantments work (valuable for mages)
- **Social Prestige:** Being seen here marks you as elite

## Services & Inventory

### Primary Services

**Equipment Sales:**
- Very Rare magic weapons/armor (5,000-50,000gp)
- Legendary items (50,000gp+, usually unique pieces)
- All prices negotiable based on item's story and intended wielder
- Payment plans available for proven heroes (Kaelith cares weapons reach worthy hands)

**Master Enchanting:**
- Permanent weapon enchantments (+1 to +3, special abilities)
- Custom enchantment design (work with Kaelith to specify properties, DM approval)
- Enchantment transfer (move enchantment from one item to another, 75% original cost)
- Enhancement of existing magic items (add additional properties, very expensive)

**Identification & Appraisal:**
- Complete item identification: 100gp (automatically succeeds, includes full history)
- Magical theory explanation (how enchantment functions): Included free
- Authenticity verification (detect fakes/forgeries): 50gp

**Restoration & Repair:**
- Legendary item repair: 10% of item's value (minimum 500gp)
- Broken magic item restoration: 25-75% original cost depending on damage
- Curse removal from weapons: 500gp base (more for complex curses)
- Attunement assistance (help understand item's powers): 200gp

**Guild Membership:**
- Cost: 5,000gp initial, 500gp/year thereafter
- Benefits:
  - Priority access to new inventory
  - 10% discount on all purchases
  - Custom commission slots (6-month wait instead of multi-year)
  - Private training room access
  - Consultation services included
  - Network of elite warriors (potential allies/hirelings)

## DM Notes

### Plot Hooks

**The Shapeshifting Project:**
- Kaelith's current obsession: perfecting adaptive weapon enchantment
- Needs rare materials: essence from shapeshifter (werewolf, doppelganger, etc.)
- Also needs field data: How do warriors adjust tactics when weapon form changes mid-combat?
- Reward for assistance: Prototype Blade of Glory (enhanced version, unique properties)
- Difficulty: Moderate-High (acquiring materials dangerous, testing requires real combat)

**The Rival Enchanter:**
- New shop in Noble Quarter producing "equivalent quality" at half price
- Kaelith skeptical - either using inferior methods or dangerous shortcuts
- Rumors suggest items contain hidden flaws/curses discovered after purchase
- Needs investigation: Are they frauds, using forbidden techniques, or legitimately innovative?
- Reward: 20% discount on future purchases, introduction to Council's security officials
- Difficulty: Moderate (investigation, possible confrontation with rival's backers)

**The Stolen Prototype:**
- Experimental weapon disappeared from forge
- Either industrial espionage or worse - design could be weaponized if replicated
- Kaelith concerned it's incomplete, might malfunction catastrophically
- Tracking magical signature difficult - thieves knew how to shield it
- Reward: Favor from Kaelith (redeemable for major service), potential keep recovered prototype
- Difficulty: High (thieves skilled, likely chaos cult or rival nation agents)

**The Legendary Commission:**
- Wealthy patron (or Council itself) commissions ultimate weapon
- Requires materials from multiple extremely dangerous locations
- Timeline compresses if party can recover materials quickly
- Kaelith offers party first pick of one custom enchantment (within reason) if they help
- Reward: Custom enchanted item, significant payment, prestige
- Difficulty: Very High (materials from high-level threats)

### Personality Quirks

- Refers to weapons as "she/her" or "he/him" based on personality perceived during crafting
- Can't resist explaining enchantment theory even when customer just wants to buy
- Taste-tests metals (claims can assess purity by flavor)
- Maintains personal combat practice despite scholarly focus (surprises challengers)
- Keeps journal of every weapon created, tracks their fates obsessively
- Becomes melancholy when legendary weapons are lost or destroyed
- Refuses to create weapons for causes she considers unjust

### Secret Information

**The Anti-Chaos Arsenal:**
- Hidden forge section developing weapons specifically harmful to chaos-touched
- Council-funded black project, highly classified
- Enchantments incorporate principles opposite to chaos magic
- Some designs dangerously unstable - fighting fire with fire creates risks
- Party discovering this reveals Kaelith's deeper involvement in anti-chaos operations

**The First Blade:**
- Kaelith's original masterwork, never sold
- Sentient weapon with whom she maintains relationship
- Serves as advisor and quality control ("Would you trust your life to this?")
- Extremely powerful, only drawn in true emergencies
- Knows secrets about Kaelith's past she doesn't discuss

**The Price of Perfection:**
- Kaelith's obsession occasionally drives her to dangerous extremes
- Has experimented with chaos magic theory to understand its opposite
- This flirtation with forbidden knowledge worries colleagues
- She believes understanding chaos necessary to defeat it (dangerous reasoning)
- Walking edge where brilliance borders on obsession

**Noble Connections:**
- Several Noble Quarter families commission exclusively from Kaelith
- These relationships create political obligations she'd prefer to avoid
- Sometimes pressured to prioritize influential clients over worthy heroes
- Struggles balancing commercial success with personal principles
