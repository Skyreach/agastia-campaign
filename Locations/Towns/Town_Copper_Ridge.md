# Copper Ridge

## Player Summary

**Copper Ridge** is a mining settlement built on a hillside rich with copper deposits. The settlement supplies copper ore and refined metal to [[Agastia]]'s craftspeople and has a rough, industrial character. Mine shafts and smelting operations dominate the landscape.

- **Type:** Mining settlement
