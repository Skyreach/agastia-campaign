# The Weeping Stones

## Player Summary

**The Weeping Stones** are an ancient stone monument of unknown origin, consisting of tall standing stones arranged in a circle. The stones "weep" - moisture constantly runs down their surfaces regardless of weather, pooling at their bases. Local folklore attributes various mystical properties and warnings to the site.

### Overview
- **Type:** Ancient monument / landmark
- **Age:** Ancient (centuries or millennia old)
- **Appearance:** Circle of 7-12 tall standing stones
- **Phenomenon:** Stones constantly weep moisture

## Folklore & Legends

**Local Stories:**

**Version 1: Memorial**
- Stones mark site of ancient tragedy
- Weeping represents eternal mourning
- Disturbing site brings bad luck
- Respect the dead and leave offerings

**Version 2: Warning**
- Stones warn of danger or prophecy
- When weeping increases, trouble comes
- Ancient civilization's warning system
- Heed the signs or face consequences

**Version 3: Magic**
- Ley line nexus or power source
- Druids and mages drawn here
- Rituals performed during celestial events
- Source of magical energy or knowledge

**Version 4: Curse**
- Stones imprison evil entity
- Weeping is entity's attempts to escape
- Never disturb or break the circle
- Doing so releases terrible doom
