# Kaelborn - Bandit Network Boss

**Kaelborn** is the ruthless and cunning leader of the Bandit Network, a powerful criminal organization operating within and around [[Agastia]]. He commands significant loyalty through a mix of fear and strategic rewards. Mentioned 44 times in Session 2.

**Appearance:** A burly human with a scarred face and a stern, calculating gaze. He often wears fine, yet practical, armor, indicating his position. Carries a well-maintained, intimidating greatsword.

**Personality:** Intelligent, ruthless, and highly ambitious. Values loyalty above all else but is quick to eliminate those who betray or undermine him. He is a strategic thinker, always planning his next move to expand the network's influence and wealth.

**Role:** The undisputed leader of the Bandit Network, overseeing all major operations, recruitment, and strategic decisions. He is the central figure in the criminal underworld of [[Agastia]].

**Current Location:** Kaelborn moves frequently, utilizing a network of safe houses and hidden strongholds throughout [[Agastia]] and its surrounding wilderness. He is rarely found in one place for long.

**Combat (Estimated Level 8-9):** AC 18 (Plate Armor), HP ~90, Greatsword (2d6+4 damage), Multiattack, Battle Master maneuvers (disarming attack, pushing attack), Intimidation (advantage on checks), adept at commanding his forces in combat.

## DM Notes

**Mastermind of the Bandit Network:**
- Controls a significant portion of [[Agastia]]'s illicit trade and criminal activities.
- Has eyes and ears throughout the city, making him difficult to track or surprise.
- Highly intelligent and anticipates counter-moves.

**Ruthless Leader:**
- Does not tolerate failure or insubordination.
- Uses fear and intimidation as primary tools for control.
- Has a few trusted lieutenants, such as Geist, who manage day-to-day operations.

**Plot Hooks:**
- The ultimate target for dismantling the Bandit Network.
- May be encountered leading a major heist or defending one of his strongholds.
- Could be involved in political intrigue, blackmailing city officials.
- Offers significant rewards for capturing or eliminating rivals.

**Loyalty Status:** Absolute loyalty from his core followers, enforced by fear and reputation.
