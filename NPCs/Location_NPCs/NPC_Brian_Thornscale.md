# Brian Thornscale

## Player Summary

**Brian Thornscale** is the mayor of [[Meridian's Rest]], a competent administrator who coordinates with the [[Merit Council]] to protect his town. While not a fighter himself, Brian is respected for his organizational skills and dedication to his community. He hired the party to clear the Ratterdan corruption and pays fairly for services rendered.

### Basic Information
- **Race:** Dragonborn (brass)
- **Gender:** Male
- **Age:** Middle-aged (50s)
- **Occupation:** Mayor of [[Meridian's Rest]]
- **Location:** [[Meridian's Rest]] (Town Hall)
- **Personality:** Competent, fair, administrative, non-combatant

### Appearance
- Brass-scaled dragonborn with dull golden hue
- Formal robes of office (simple but well-maintained)
- Wears mayoral medallion
- Neat and organized appearance
- Carries ledgers and official documents
- Not physically imposing despite dragonborn heritage

### Demeanor
- Professional and bureaucratic (but not cold)
- Fair-minded in disputes
- Organized and methodical
- Relies on others for combat/security (knows his limits)
- Appreciative of capable adventurers
- Defensive of his town and people

### Known Activities
- Administers [[Meridian's Rest]] governance
- Coordinates with [[Merit Council]] representatives
- Handles town budget and resource allocation
- Resolves disputes between citizens
- Hires adventurers for problems beyond town guard capability
- Works closely with [[Assistant Bellweather]] (his aide)
- Defers to [[Thorne Kallister]] for defense matters

### Personality & Behavior
- Competent administrator, not a warrior
- Pays fairly and promptly for services
- Values law, order, and community safety
- Pragmatic about using adventurers for dangerous work
- Grateful to capable problem-solvers
- Doesn't take credit for others' work

## Knowledge & Information

### About [[Meridian's Rest]]
- Population ~2000 (half transient travelers)
- Function: Resupply hub for [[Agastia]]-bound adventurers
- Services available: Inn, apothecary, scribe, blacksmith, general goods
- Defenses: Town guard (10 guards) + volunteer militia
- Threats: Moon's Assault (periodic), road bandits, monsters

### About the Region
- [[Agastia]] is 2 days travel south (48 miles)
- Roads generally safe near [[Meridian's Rest]]
- Trade caravans pass through regularly
- Ratterdan corruption now cleared (thanks to party)

### About [[Merit Council]]
- [[Meridian's Rest]] operates under [[Merit Council]] jurisdiction
- Pays taxes and receives occasional support
- [[Merit Council]] sometimes funds adventurer contracts
- Relationship is professional but distant
