# Thorne "The Bear" Kallister

## Player Summary

**Thorne "The Bear" Kallister** is a retired adventurer who made [[Meridian's Rest]] his home after decades of dungeon delving. Broad-shouldered with a thick salt-and-pepper beard and a slight limp from an old injury, Thorne evaluates newcomers carefully before trusting them. He offers quests to capable adventurers and guards a painful secret: the death of his friend Garrek twenty years ago, and the mystery of what Garrek found in the Underdark.

### Basic Information
- **Race:** Human
- **Gender:** Male
- **Age:** Late 50s
- **Occupation:** Retired adventurer, informal quest giver
- **Location:** [[Meridian's Rest]] (resident)
- **Personality:** Gruff but kind, protective, evaluative, haunted by past loss

### Appearance
- Broad-shouldered, muscular build (going to muscle-and-fat with age)
- Thick salt-and-pepper beard, weathered face
- Slight limp from old injury (left leg)
- Wears practical clothes (leather vest, sturdy boots)
- Multiple scars visible on arms and hands
- Often seen at the tavern, watching newcomers

### Demeanor
- Gruff exterior hiding kindness
- Evaluates people before trusting them
- Protective of [[Meridian's Rest]] community
- Serious when discussing threats
- Softens when talking about old adventuring days
- Haunted expression when mentioning Garrek

### Known Activities
- Defends [[Meridian's Rest]] during Moon's Assault events
- Hires capable adventurers for local problems
- Drinks at tavern, shares war stories
- Watches the road (old habits die hard)
- Mentors young fighters occasionally
- Keeps Garrek's journal hidden (until trust earned)

### Personality & Behavior
- Tests newcomers' capability and character before trusting
- Values competence and teamwork in combat
- Protective of townsfolk (will fight alongside defenders)
- Shares personal information only after trust earned
- Philosophical about aging and loss
- Still feels guilt over Garrek's death
- Won't send people into danger without knowing they can handle it

## Relationships

### Old Adventuring Party
- **Garrek (Deceased):** Best friend, dwarven explorer who found [[Garrek's Falls]]
  - Died 20 years ago in Underdark
  - Thorne never learned full circumstances
  - Kept Garrek's journal all these years
  - Feels guilt over not being there
- **Torvan:** Party member mentioned in Garrek's journal
  - Lost finger, regrown by healing waters
  - Became addicted to waters (Garrek had to drag him away)
  - Current whereabouts unknown
- **Others:** Thorne had a full party "back in the day"

### [[Meridian's Rest]] Community

  - Mayor relies on Thorne for defense coordination
  - Thorne handles adventurer recruitment
- **[[Assistant Bellweather]]:** Professional relationship (Mayor's assistant)

### Potential Allies
- **Party:** If they prove competent and trustworthy
  - Tests them with Quest 1 (combat capability)
  - Tests them with Quest 2 (character under fire)

## Quests Offered

### Quest 1: Monster Hunt (50gp)

**Purpose:** Test party's combat capability

**Hook:**
> "You handled that corruption well. The town's grateful. But we've got another problem - creature on the trade road. Merchants won't travel until it's gone. 50 gold if you take care of it."

**Objective:** Kill or drive off creature threatening trade road

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- How does party work together in combat?
- Do they use tactics or just charge in?
- Do they protect each other?
- Are they competent enough for harder tasks?

**Creature Options (DM Choice):**
- Displacer Beast (CR 3)
- Owlbear (CR 3)
- Young Basilisk (CR 2)

### Quest 2: Defend Against Moon's Assault (No Pay)

**Purpose:** Test party's character under pressure

**Hook:**
> "You fight well. Good. Because in two nights, we'll need every blade. The Moon's Assault - happens every few months. Lycanthropes and berserkers come down from the hills. We defend the walls or people die."
>
> "There's no pay for this. Just survival."

**Objective:** Defend [[Meridian's Rest]] from attacking forces

**Thorne's Role During Defense:**
- Fights alongside party (limited by old injury/limp)
- Provides tactical advice
- Shields weaker party members if endangered
- Observes how party handles pressure

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Do they fight for community or flee?
- How do they handle civilians in danger?
- Do they work with Thorne and town defenders?
- Will they risk themselves for others?

**Enemy Composition:**
- Wave 1: 6 Berserkers (CR 2 total) - East gate
- Wave 2: 2 Werewolves (CR 3 each) + 4 Wolves - North gate
- Final Wave: 1 Werebear (CR 5) - Final push

### Quest 3: Investigate Garrek's Falls (100gp + Artifacts)

**Purpose:** Solve the mystery of Garrek's death, learn the truth

**Prerequisite:** Party must complete Quests 1 & 2 successfully

**Hook:**
> After the Moon's Assault, Thorne takes you aside. He seems conflicted.
>
> "You've earned my trust. So I'll share something personal. Twenty years ago, my party explored the Underdark. My friend Garrek found a waterfall - said it had healing properties. Miraculous healing. But he died before we could return."
>
> He hands you a partially burned journal.
>
> "I want to know what happened to him. What he found. 100 gold for the truth. Plus... whatever Garrek left behind is yours."

**Garrek's Journal Contents:**
- Map fragment showing route to [[Garrek's Falls]]
- Entry 1: "The waters heal anything. Regrew Torvan's finger in minutes."
- Entry 2: "Strange. Time seems... wrong near the Falls. Clocks run backward."
- Entry 3: "Found a book. Seven pages, metal, bound to a pedestal. Can't read it."
- Entry 4: "Torvan tried to drink more water. Had to drag him away. ADDICTIVE."
- Entry 5 (burned): "...tribe guards it... temporal... don't trust..."

**What Party Discovers at [[Garrek's Falls]]:**
- Healing aura and waters (miraculous but ADDICTIVE)
- [[The Codex]] of Peace (7 metal pages, bound to pedestal)
- [[Temporal Bloodline Tribe]] (guardians)
- Evidence of what happened to Garrek (DM to determine)
- Temporal anomalies (time flows strangely)

**Reward:**
- 100gp payment
- "Whatever Garrek left behind" (DM choice of artifacts)
- Garrek's journal (keep it)
- Knowledge of Codex location (critical for [[Manny]]'s quest)
