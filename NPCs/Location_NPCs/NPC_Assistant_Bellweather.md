# Assistant Bellweather

## Player Summary

**Assistant Bellweather** is the mayor's assistant in [[Meridian's Rest]], handling the day-to-day logistics of town administration. Organized, efficient, and discreet, Bellweather ensures Mayor [[Brian Thornscale]]'s office runs smoothly. They are the person to see for official town business, scheduling, and administrative questions.

### Basic Information
- **Race:** Half-Elf
- **Gender:** Unspecified (uses they/them pronouns)
- **Age:** 30s
- **Occupation:** Mayor's Assistant / Administrative Aide
- **Location:** [[Meridian's Rest]] (Town Hall)
- **Personality:** Organized, efficient, professional, discreet

### Appearance
- Half-elf with neat appearance
- Practical business attire (tunic, trousers, vest)
- Hair pulled back efficiently
- Carries ledger and quill at all times
- Wears official town clerk badge
- Always looks busy but never flustered

### Demeanor
- Professional and courteous
- Efficient without being curt
- Detail-oriented and organized
- Protective of mayor's time
- Patient with confused citizens
- Slightly harried during busy periods

### Known Activities
- Manages Mayor [[Brian Thornscale]]'s schedule
- Handles town paperwork and filing
- Greets visitors to Town Hall
- Coordinates town meetings and announcements
- Maintains town records (with [[Aldwin Quill]]'s help)
- Liaises with [[Merit Council]] representatives
- Posts public notices and bounties

## Role in Town Administration

### Primary Responsibilities
- **Scheduling:** Manages mayor's appointments
- **Records:** Files and organizes official documents
- **Communications:** Handles correspondence
- **Coordination:** Arranges town meetings, events
- **Public Interface:** First point of contact at Town Hall

### Authority Level
- Can answer routine administrative questions
- Cannot make policy decisions (refers to mayor)
- Has access to public records
- Can approve minor expenditures (<5gp)
- Trusted with confidential information

## Interaction with Party

### If Party Visits Town Hall
**Bellweather's Response:**
- Greets party professionally
- Asks nature of business
- Can answer questions about town services
- Schedules meeting with mayor if needed
- Provides directions to shops, services
- Posts bounties or notices if requested

### Information Available
**Public Knowledge (Free):**
- Town services and locations
- Public meeting schedules
- Posted bounties or notices
- General town information

**Official Records (Requires Reason):**
- Property ownership (public record)
- Birth/death records (public with restrictions)
- Town ordinances and laws
- [[Merit Council]] decrees affecting town

**Restricted Information (Mayor's Approval):**
- Confidential correspondences
- Sensitive town matters
- Private citizen information

## Knowledge & Information

### About [[Meridian's Rest]]
- Knows every service, shop, and resident
- Can direct party to exactly what they need
- Aware of town schedule and events
- Knows who to ask for specialized information

### About Mayor [[Brian Thornscale]]
- Completely loyal and trustworthy
- Knows his schedule and priorities
- Can speak for him on routine matters
- Protects his time from non-urgent issues

### About [[Merit Council]]
- Handles official communications
- Knows requirements for permits, travel papers
- Aware of current [[Merit Council]] policies
- Can explain bureaucratic processes

## Relationships

- Bellweather protects his time and interests

### Town Officials
- **[[Thorne Kallister]]:** Professional respect (knows his importance)

- **[[Aldwin Quill]]:** Works with him on official documents
- **[[Greta Moss]]:** Friendly acquaintance

### Citizens
- Known and respected by all residents
- Patient with complaints and questions
- Fair and impartial in official capacity

### Adventurers

  - Can help with administrative needs

  - Thanks them for clearing corruption (if mentioned)

## DM Notes

- **Administrative Helper:** Answers routine questions
- **Information Gatekeeper:** Controls access to mayor
- **Town Directory:** Knows where everything is
- **Efficient NPC:** Moves party along to what they need

### Why This NPC Matters
- Represents competent bureaucracy
- Provides information without main NPC needed
- Realistic town administration
- Helpful without being plot-critical

### Personality Notes
- Professional and organized
- Neither friendly nor cold (businesslike)
- Efficient problem-solver
- Discreet about sensitive information
- Patient with well-meaning confusion
- Impatient with time-wasters

### Usage Tips
**When Party Needs:**
- Directions to town services → Bellweather provides
- Meeting with mayor → Bellweather schedules
- Town information → Bellweather knows it
- Official paperwork → Bellweather handles it

**When to Use Mayor Instead:**
- Policy decisions
- Official town positions
- Major announcements
- Quest payments (already handled by [[Thava Thornscale]])

### Future Hooks
- Recurring administrative contact
- Can post party's notices or bounties
- Connection to [[Merit Council]] bureaucracy

## Secrets
None. Bellweather is discreet but honest.

## Combat Stats
**None.** Bellweather is a non-combatant.

**If Town Hall Attacked:**
- Flees to safety or hides
- Tries to protect important documents
- Relies on town guard for protection
- May try to get mayor to safety

## Administrative Services

### What Bellweather Can Help With
1. **Scheduling:** Arrange meeting with mayor or officials
2. **Directions:** "Where is the apothecary/scribe/etc?"
3. **Records:** Access public documents
4. **Posting:** Put up party's notice or bounty
5. **Permits:** Explain requirements for official papers
6. **Questions:** General town information

### What Requires Mayor
1. **Policy:** Town decisions or ordinances
2. **Payments:** Official contracts (already handled)
3. **Disputes:** Legal matters between citizens
4. **Representation:** Official town positions

## Quick Dialogue Examples

**Party Enters Town Hall:**
> Bellweather looks up from their ledger.
>
> "Good day. How may I help you?"

**Party Asks for Mayor:**
> "Mayor Thornscale is [busy/available]. May I ask the nature of your business?"
>
> [If routine] "I can help you with that."
>
> [If important] "Let me see if he's available."

**Party Asks for Directions:**
> "Certainly. The apothecary is two streets over, near the market square. Ask for Greta Moss. Tell her the Town Hall sent you."

**Party Thanks for Corruption Clearing:**
> Bellweather smiles briefly.
>
> "The town is grateful. Is there anything else I can assist you with today?"
