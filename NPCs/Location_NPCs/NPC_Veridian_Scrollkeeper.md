# Veridian Scrollkeeper

## Player Summary

**Veridian Scrollkeeper** is an elderly half-elf scholar who has dedicated his life to studying ancient civilizations and lost artifacts. He works at the [[Archive of Lost Histories]] in [[Agastia]]'s [[Scholar Quarter]], surrounded by dusty tomes and fragmented texts. Known for his enthusiasm when discussing ancient lore, Veridian loves sharing knowledge with anyone genuinely interested.

### Basic Information
- **Race:** Half-Elf
- **Gender:** Male
- **Age:** Elderly (appears ~80-90, but half-elf aging means could be 150+)
- **Occupation:** Artifact Scholar / Archivist
- **Location:** [[Archive of Lost Histories]], [[Scholar Quarter]], [[Agastia]]
- **Personality:** Enthusiastic, generous with knowledge, slightly absent-minded

### Appearance
- Thin frame, slightly stooped from years bent over books
- Silver-white hair pulled back in a simple tail
- Uses ornate walking stick (gift from former student)
- Reading spectacles perpetually perched on nose
- Ink stains on fingers from decades of research
- Simple scholar's robes in faded blue

### Demeanor
- Lights up when discussing ancient artifacts
- Speaks rapidly when excited about a topic
- Sometimes trails off mid-sentence when remembering a reference
- Will retrieve books mid-conversation to verify details
- Patient with genuine seekers of knowledge
- Impatient with those who waste his time

### Known Activities
- Works daily at [[Archive of Lost Histories]]
- Researches pre-[[Merit Council]] artifacts and civilizations
- Advises scholars seeking historical information
- Has spent 40+ years studying peace-binding artifacts
- Known in academic circles as leading expert on ancient items
- Maintains extensive personal notes and cross-references

### Personality & Behavior
- Enthusiastic and generous with knowledge
- Becomes animated when discussing topics of interest
- Absent-minded about practical matters
- Patient teacher to genuine students
- Dismissive of treasure hunters and grave robbers
- Values knowledge preservation over profit

## Knowledge Base

### Area of Expertise
**Ancient Civilizations & Artifacts:**
- Pre-[[Merit Council]] history (specialty)
- Lost magical items and their properties
- Ancient defensive magic and binding rituals
- Temporal anomalies and time-related artifacts
- Underdark civilizations and their remnants

### The Dominion Evolution Codex

**What Veridian Knows:**
- Studied legends of "peace-binding artifacts" for 40+ years
- Found references to a "Seven-Page Codex" in fragmentary texts
- **Ancient Text Reference:** "Book of Seven Metals, bound to stone eternal"
- **Properties:** Said to enforce peace itself - prevents violence in its presence
- **Location Clues:** Hidden in sanctuary where "time flows strangely"
- **Healing Waters:** Old explorer journals mention miraculous healing
- **Guardians:** Temporal Bloodline Tribe mentioned as keepers
- **Warning:** One text warned watchers must be "pure of heart and clear of purpose"

**Sources:**
- Fragment from pre-Council tome (Archive reference: LC-4072)
- Explorer journal dated 180 years ago (partial, water-damaged)
- Oral traditions collected from Underdark traders
- Cross-referenced symbols matching Codex descriptions

### Other Relevant Knowledge
- Familiar with Garrek's reputation (famous explorer, presumed dead 20 years ago)
- Knows general Underdark geography (academic knowledge)
- Can identify temporal magic effects when described
- Understands ancient binding rituals and their purposes
