# Greta Moss

## Player Summary

**Greta Moss** runs the apothecary in [[Meridian's Rest]], providing potions, poisons, and alchemical ingredients to travelers and adventurers. Grateful to the party for clearing the Ratterdan corruption, she offers discounted materials and crafting services to those who helped protect the town.

### Basic Information
- **Race:** Human
- **Gender:** Female
- **Age:** Late 40s
- **Occupation:** Apothecary / Alchemist
- **Location:** [[Meridian's Rest]] (Apothecary Shop)
- **Personality:** Practical, knowledgeable, grateful, herbalist

### Appearance
- Graying brown hair tied back with herb-stained ribbon
- Practical work dress with many pockets
- Herb and chemical scents follow her
- Stained fingers from grinding ingredients
- Reading glasses on chain around neck
- Warm smile, laugh lines around eyes

### Demeanor
- Practical and no-nonsense about her craft
- Friendly with regular customers
- Generous to those who help the community
- Patient teacher if asked about herbs
- Protective of her shop and ingredients

## Services Offered

### Standard Goods (Normal Prices)
- **Healing Potions:** 50gp (2d4+2 HP)
- **Antitoxin:** 50gp
- **Alchemist's Fire:** 50gp
- **Potion of Climbing:** 75gp
- **Potion of Healing (Greater):** 150gp (4d4+4 HP)
- **Acid (vial):** 25gp
- **Basic Poison:** 100gp
- **Herbalism Kit:** 5gp
- **Components:** Various prices

### Crafting Services
**For Party (15-20% Discount on Materials):**
- **Healing Potion:** 1 day, 25gp in materials (or 12gp with gathered moonbell flowers)
- **Greater Healing Potion:** 2 days, 100gp in materials (or 50gp with silverleaf + cave crystals)
- **Antitoxin:** 1 day, 50gp (or 25gp with ingredients)
- **Alchemist's Fire:** 1 day, 50gp

### Ingredient Gathering (Guidance Provided)
**Moonbell Flowers:**
- Medicine or Nature DC 12
- 1d4 hours search time
- Nearby forest locations
- Used in healing potions

**Silverleaf:**
- Survival DC 14
- 2d4 hours search time
- Deep forest locations
- Used in greater healing potions

**Cave Crystals:**
- Investigation DC 13
- 2d4 hours search time
- Local cave systems
- Used in greater healing potions

## Knowledge & Information

### Herbalism & Alchemy
- Expert knowledge of local plants
- Can identify herbs, fungi, natural toxins
- Knows antidotes for common poisons
- Can advise on potion brewing techniques

### Local Resources
- Knows where to gather ingredients near [[Meridian's Rest]]
- Can recommend safe gathering locations
- Warns about dangerous areas (corrupted groves, monster lairs)
- Shares seasonal availability of herbs

### Town Information
- Knows most residents (medical care provider)
- Can share gossip about health-related matters
- Aware of recent injuries, illnesses, treatments
- Discretion varies by topic (some medical privacy)

## Relationships

  - Both offer crafting services
  - Refer customers to each other
  - Share tea occasionally

### Town Officials
- **[[Brian Thornscale]]:** Professional relationship (supplies town guard with antitoxins)
- **[[Thorne Kallister]]:** Shares herb lore, supplies him with healing salves

- **Party:** Grateful for clearing corruption
  - Offers 15-20% discount on materials
  - Will help with crafting projects
  - Shares ingredient gathering locations

## DM Notes

### Greta's Role
- **Crafting Hub:** Provides downtime crafting services
- **Resource Management:** Offers discount as quest reward
- **Practical Helper:** No-nonsense shopkeeper who helps party prepare
- **Recurring Contact:** Available for future potion needs

### Why the Discount?
- Town is grateful for corruption clearing
- Greta personally appreciates safety improvement
- Corruption threatened local herb supplies
- Goodwill toward capable adventurers

### Personality Notes
- Practical herbalist, not mystical
- Knowledge-focused (can teach about ingredients)
- Fair prices, honest dealings
- Generous to town protectors
- Businesswoman but community-minded

### Crafting Mechanics
- Uses standard D&D crafting rules
- Discount applies to material costs only (not labor time)
- Gathering ingredients can reduce costs further
- Party can use her shop during downtime periods

### Future Hooks
- Regular potion supplier
- Can identify unknown alchemical substances
- May need rare ingredients gathered (quest hook)
- Connection to herbalism and natural magic

## Secrets
**Minor Secret:** Knows more about poisons than she admits publicly
- Sells to hunters and trappers quietly
- Not illegal, just sensitive
- Won't make deadly poisons for just anyone

## Combat Stats
**None.** Greta is a non-combatant.

**If Shop Attacked:**
- Flees or hides (no combat capability)
- May throw alchemical items in desperation
- Relies on town guard for protection
