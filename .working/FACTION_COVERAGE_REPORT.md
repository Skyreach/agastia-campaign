# Faction Coverage Analysis: Agastia City
**Date:** 2025-12-14
**Scope:** Cross-reference of all major factions with their physical presence in Agastia City

---

## Executive Summary

This report validates faction coverage in Agastia City by cross-referencing faction files with location, NPC, and establishment files. Analysis reveals **strong coverage for most major factions**, with clear headquarters, NPCs, and district presence. However, several **gaps exist** for specific factions, particularly the Decimate Project and Bandit Network.

### Coverage Status Overview
- **Merit Council:** ✅ COMPLETE - Extensive coverage
- **Chaos Cult:** ✅ COMPLETE - Well-integrated
- **Dispossessed:** ✅ COMPLETE - Strong presence
- **Merchant Guild:** ✅ COMPLETE - Clear headquarters
- **Decimate Project:** ⚠️ PARTIAL - Limited city presence
- **Bandit Network (Geist & Kaelborn):** ⚠️ MINIMAL - No established presence

## Detailed Faction Analysis

### 1. Merit Council ✅ COMPLETE

**Headquarters/Locations:**
- **Primary:** Government Complex (Tier 4) - Public-facing operations
- **Secondary:** The Castle/Inverse Tower (Tier 1) - 13 underground levels, restricted access
  - Council chambers (public levels 1-3)
  - Administrative offices (levels 4-7)
  - Restricted archives (levels 8-10)
  - Sealed levels (11-13) - forbidden materials

**Physical Presence in Districts:**
- Government Complex: Main administrative center
- The Castle: Political/military headquarters
- Scholar Quarter: Testing centers, merit certification facilities
- All districts: City Watch patrols, Testing Enforcers

**Representative NPCs:**
- High Councilor Mareth Brightforge (Government Complex)
- Security Director Kael Ironscript (Investigation Department)
- Archivist-General Senna Vale (Merit Registry)
- Master Examiner Thane Correct (Testing system)
- Councilor Rhen Darkwood (Reform faction)
- Inspector Crane (Merchant District enforcement)
- Examiner-General Krite (Scholar Quarter testing)

**Mentioned in Files:**
- `/Locations/Districts/Government_Complex.md` - Extensive detail
- `/Locations/Districts/The_Castle.md` - Headquarters
- `/Locations/Cities/Agastia_City.md` - Governing faction
- Multiple shop and district files reference enforcement presence

**Coverage Assessment:** EXCELLENT - Merit Council has clear headquarters, multiple sub-locations, named NPCs at various levels, and presence across all city tiers.

- **Questions:**

**RECOMMENDATION:**
- Clarify relationship in `/Locations/Cities/Agastia/Shop_Brightcoin_Emergency_Supplies.md`
- If separate: Create Aldara's establishment (emergency supply distribution network?)
- If same person: Update faction file to use correct name (Marcus)

#### 2. Bandit Network Has No Agastia Presence
- **Issue:** Faction operates "in and around Agastia" but has zero established locations
- **Impact:** Kyle's storyline requires they be present/active in city
- **Missing Elements:**
  - Safe house or hideout
  - Front business (legitimate cover)

  - Smuggling infrastructure

- Establish **Dock District safe house** (maritime smuggling route)

#### 4. Professor Zero's "Network" Undetailed

## Conclusion

**Overall Assessment:** Agastia City has **strong faction coverage** for most major factions, with clear headquarters, representative NPCs, and district integration. However, **two critical gaps require attention:**

1. **Aldara/Marcus Brightcoin name inconsistency** (Chaos Cult connection)
2. **Bandit Network lacks any Agastia presence** (despite operating "in and around" city)

All other factions have appropriate representation:
- Merit Council: Extensive (multiple locations, many NPCs)
- Dispossessed: Excellent (The Depths well-developed)
- Merchant Guild: Complete (The Gilded Scale detailed)
- Chaos Cult: Good (covert presence appropriate for faction type)
- Decimate Project: Appropriate (external faction, limited presence intentional)

**Recommended Next Steps:**
1. Clarify Brightcoin character situation
2. Establish Bandit Network operational base in Dock District
3. Create front business and corrupt NPC connections for bandits
4. Connect to Kyle's backstory (intercepted message location)
