# [[The Codex]] of Peace

## Player Summary

**The Codex of Peace** (also known as the **Dominion Evolution Codex**) is an ancient tome of immense power, sought by [[Professor Zero]] for unknown purposes. The artifact is said to contain knowledge of evolution, transformation, and change â€” magic that grants dominion over life and growth itself. Legends describe it as impossibly well-preserved despite its age, with pages that appear blank until read by a "worthy" individual.

[[Manny]] (#1) and [[Nikki]] (#2) have been dispatched by [[Professor Zero]] to locate and retrieve the Codex, though its true contents and purpose remain unknown to them. Garrek's journal mentions an "ancient book at the Falls," potentially referencing this artifact.

### Known Properties
- **Ancient Knowledge:** Contains evolution/transformation magic
- **Protected Location:** Hidden at [[Garrek's Falls]] (Underdark sanctuary)
- **Magical Defense:** Creates zone preventing violence, ill will, and evil acts
- **Selective Revelation:** Pages appear blank except to "worthy" readers
- **Unique Construction:** Bound in material that is neither leather nor metal
- **Inner Light:** Glows with faint luminescence
