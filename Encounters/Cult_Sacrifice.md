# Cult Sacrifice

**Roll:** 10 on 2d6

Torchlight flickers through the mist. A cult fanatic and robed followers prepare to sacrifice a terrified kobold on a stone altar to a dark god. The kobold is somehow immune to piercing damage and keeps screaming.

**Variation:** The kobold is actually a polymorphed noble, or the cult serves a black dragon who watches from nearby.
